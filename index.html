OUR VERDICT

Brilliant

Okay, so there’s a lot to unpack here. If you’re not familiar with rejuvenating sets, they’re basically sets of four or five products that promise whiter, brighter skin — the perfect glass skin, if you will. But they’re also quite controversial for containing too many active ingredients, and even tricky ones like hydroquinone and tretinoin, which require the careful supervision of dermatologists. So why do people love rejuv sets so much? For one thing, they’re incredibly affordable. One set complete with 5 different skincare products can cost the same as, let’s say, a single cleanser that will take months to produce visible effects. But while rejuv sets act fast, plenty of dermatologists say they’re not great for long-term use. They’re also not recommended for someone with sensitive skin. So is the Brilliant Skin rejuv set any different? My take: not really. I was hesitant to try it and after a few days I already noticed mild irritation and redness in certain areas. The toner especially was harsh. The sunblock and night cream I didn’t really mind.
